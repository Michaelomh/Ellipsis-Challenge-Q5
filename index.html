3 Days of Fun & Bonding
22th - 24th July 2018
This year, over 350 freshmen will be joining the close-knitted SIS family! A camp organized to expose and integrate freshies into SIS culture, one can expect to forge lasting friendships that would impact their university experience.

Meta Camp 2018 will span across 3D2N, with activities and games conducted at SMU and Sentosa Island. Get ready for the BIGGEST, BOLDEST, and BEST camp in town!
